#  Sales Performance Dashboard (Task 1)

##  Overview

This project analyzes business sales data to identify key trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The goal is to provide actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Dataset

* Source: Superstore Sales Dataset
* Contains information on:

  * Orders and dates
  * Products and categories
  * Sales, profit, and quantity
  * Regions and customer segments

---

##  Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization (Charts)

---

##  Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue Trend Over Time

* Analyzed yearly sales performance
* Identified overall growth trends

### 2. Top 10 Products by Revenue

* Determined highest revenue-generating products
* Highlighted key contributors to sales

### 3. Sales by Category

* Compared performance across product categories

### 4. Sales by Region

* Evaluated regional sales distribution

---

##  Key Insights

* Sales show a consistent upward trend, with strong growth after 2015
* Technology is the highest-performing category
* The Canon imageCLASS 2200 copier generates the highest revenue
* The West region leads in total sales

## Conclusion

This analysis highlights important business trends and provides insights that can help improve decision-making, optimize product focus, and identify high-performing regions.
